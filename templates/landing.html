Yes. Cancel from your Settings page with one click. Your access continues until the end of your billing period. No contracts or cancellation fees.

What is the Opportunity Score?

A 0-99 composite score measuring affiliate potential. It factors in sales demand, momentum, commission rate, creator saturation gap, and price sweet spot to surface the best opportunities.

What are TAP Boosted Links?

TAP (The Affiliate Program) links offer higher commission rates on select products through partner programs. You earn more per sale compared to standard TikTok Shop commissions.

How is Vantage different from Kalodata or FastMoss?

Vantage is built specifically for affiliate sellers at an accessible $19.99/month price point. We offer the same core data (sales, commissions, creators) plus unique features like AI product assistant, TAP boosted links, and an integrated Discord bot.

Do I need a TikTok Shop seller account?

No. Vantage is for affiliate marketers and content creators. You don't need a seller account — just sign up via Discord and start browsing products to promote.
