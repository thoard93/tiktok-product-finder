Privacy Policy

Last Revised: December 21, 2025

Vantage Intelligence ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our high-frequency market intelligence platform.

1. Data Acquisition

Information from Discord OAuth: When you authenticate via Discord, we acquire the following telemetry:

  • Discord User ID (Unique system identifier)
  • Username and Discriminator
  • Avatar Resource URLs
  • Verified Email Address

2. Synthetic Data Usage

We process acquired telemetry to:

  • Authorize access to the Vantage Intelligence mainframe
  • Personalize your command dashboard (e.g., product watchlists)
  • Monitor system performance and protocol integrity
  • Enforce security mandates and prevent unauthorized access

3. External Projections

Vantage does NOT sell or rent your personal intelligence. Data is only projected under the following conditions:

  • Infrastructure Partners: Secure transmission to our hosting provider (Render) and database clusters.
  • Legal Mandates: Compliance with binding judicial orders or governmental protocols.

4. Integrity & Security

All data transmissions are secured via High-Grade Encryption (HTTPS/TLS). Your records are stored in decentralized, secure database clusters with strictly limited operator access. We implement 24/7 monitoring to ensure the continuous integrity of our intelligence databanks.

5. Operator Rights

You maintain the right to access, rectify, or purge your intelligence records. To initiate a data purge protocol, please contact our community moderators via the Vantage Discord Mainframe.
